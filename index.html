🌿 ABOUT THE HACK

What is BlockVerse?

12 hours of pure, unbridled creation

BlockVerse 2026 is a fast-paced 12-hour Minecraft-themed hackathon focused on AI/ML and AR/VR, where innovation meets execution. Organised by SNJB's Late Sau Kantabai Bhavarlalji Jain College of Engineering, it brings together students to build real-world solutions under pressure.

Like Minecraft's infinite world, your imagination is the only limit. Whether you're mining through data, designing intelligent systems, or crafting immersive experiences — every block matters.

In just one day, participants will design, develop, and present impactful projects while collaborating in teams, learning from peers, and pushing their limits in a high-energy environment.

❓ CRAFTING GUIDE

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before entering the BlockVerse.

Who can participate in BlockVerse? ▶
BlockVerse is open to all college students. You can participate in teams of up to 2-4 members.
What is the selection process? ▶
After registration, teams must submit a PPT in Phase 1. Shortlisted teams will be invited to the offline hackathon round.
Is there any participation fee? ▶
Registration is free. However, shortlisted teams must pay an entry fee of ₹250 for the offline round.
What should we submit in Phase 1? ▶
A short presentation (PPT) explaining your idea, problem statement, and proposed solution.
Do we need to bring our own laptop? ▶
Yes, all participants must bring their own laptops and necessary equipment.
Will food be provided? ▶
Yes, dinner will be provided during the hackathon for all participants.
Can we start working on the project before the hackathon? ▶
You can ideate beforehand, but the actual development must be done during the hackathon.
How are projects judged? ▶
Projects are evaluated based on innovation, technical implementation, impact, and presentation.
Where will the hackathon be conducted? ▶
The offline hackathon will be conducted at our college campus. Details will be shared with shortlisted teams.
Still have questions? ▶
Reach out to us at gdg.oncampussnjb@gmail.com — we’re happy to help!
